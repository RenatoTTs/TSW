Our office is on the north east corner of Bloor Street West and Islington Avenue. Street parking is available nearby and paid parking is available underground.

Directions by public transit (TTC):
  1. Exit the subway at Islington Station on the Bloor-Danforth line
  2. As you pass by the collector booth you will see a sign for the Sun Life Financial Centre
  3. Follow that hallway and go up the escalator into the Centre’s shopping mall (Bloor-Islington Place)
  4. Stay to your left and walk straight through the shopping mall to the other end where it says “East Tower”
  5. Take the elevator up to the sixth floor and check in with our receptionist
